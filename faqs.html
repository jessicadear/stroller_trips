    FAQs

    What kinds of trips are offered?

    We have the Provence region that range from one week to three-week trips. We explore this part of the south of France on foot, bicycle, boat ride, “petit” train ride; depending on the city and region that we explore that day. We also have trips to the Alps. Where you can explore by skiing, snowboarding, trekking, wearing snowshoes, zip lining, rafting, kayaking, canoeing, riding a bike or a sled.

    How do I know what each trip is like?

    You can see pictures of each city and/or activity in our website. If you’d like full details on certain cities or activities, please e-mail us at jessicadear@me.com We would be happy to discuss our amazing trips with you!

    Who goes on Stroller Trips?

    Families! Small families, big families, grandparents with grandchildren, single parent’s, everyone is welcome to travel easy and fun enjoying every little thing, since we have the whole trip designed around each member of your family. Meaning, everyone will get to enjoy their vacation according to their age and interests. Not to mention, that we have everything that families may need from carseats and highchairs to diapers and toys in your accommodation.

    What is your maximum group size?

    We keep our groups small, usually from 1 to 3 families.

    What if I'm traveling alone with my child, do I still get a nanny?

    Of course you do. Even though it’s a family trip, mom and/or dad (legal guardians) need a time for exploring as well. So enjoy exploring your destination with the help of your personal nanny.

    Do you offer bespoke trips?

    Yes. We offer private single family trips to the locations and activities that best fits your ideal vacation.

    Who is the trip director?

    The trip director is Jessica Dear. She is a multilingual American mom of three, entrepreneur, with masters degrees in psychology and education. She has traveled around the world and has a very good idea of what families around the world would appreciate the most, since she has traveled with her family to places like China, Mexico, United States, Germany, Spain, France..

    What do I need to bring?

    You really just need to bring your passport, wallet and your love ones.

    What's the food like?

    Breakfast is included with the accommodation. We keep the breakfast simple and cozy. We’ll ask each family in advanced their preferred ingredients, fruits, cereals, and so; So each one will have that ready in their suite. Lunch and dinner will usually be at a restaurant in the town that we’re visiting that day. This gives each family the freedom to chose exactly the place and kind of food of their preference. Remember, the nanny will be with you during the lunch and dinner experience, so don’t stress over eating out with kids. Your nanny will make sure that your restaurant experience is as smooth as possible.

    Can I give a trip as a gift?

    Yes, you can purchase a specific trip and give it as a gift. We have had the cases in which parents couldn’t take off, but kids come with grandparents instead. So that is definitely an option.

    Do I need travel insurance services?

    We highly recommend that you do acquire a trip insurance of your preference since airplane delays, cancellations for various reasons, and so, are likely to happen. So yes, for those unforeseen circumstances that may arise, we strongly recommend the purchase of a traveling insurance plan. Which would pay for loss due to covered unforeseen circumstances or death, injury, or illness of you or a family member. Some traveling plans even cover emergency medical transportation and medical expenses. Look for a customized insurance policy that meets the needs of an active traveler.

    Do I have to pay for my nanny’s food?

    No, it is not required.

    If I have two or more kids, could I ask for 2 nannies?

    Yes, while that’s optional, it obviously would make the trip very smooth. (Additional costs for second nanny apply)

    What kind of activities does the nanny plan for the kids?

    Before your arrival, the trip director will have knowledge of your family’s interests, kids age, and so; So the nanny will have age appropriate activities to that day’s trip visits. For example: If you visit an art museum near Van Gogh’s place in Saint Remy de Provence, the nanny would likely have paint, colors, and canvases ready for your kids to be the mini Van Gogh of the Day at that same location where Vincent Van Gogh once got inspired to paint the Starry Night.

    Are tips included?

    The chauffeurs, nannies and trip directors have a salary; But if you wished to tip any of the staff, I’m sure they would appreciate it.

    What kind of clothes should I take?

    Your trip director will send you a detailed email with all the information that you may need to know prior to your trip. If you have specific questions after that, feel free to email us.

    How are the days planned?

    Normally, you will have time in the morning to get ready and enjoy your breakfast, and your chauffeur, trip director and nanny, will pick you up and take you to the city / activity of the day. There you will have lunch, enjoy activities, then have dinner, and then back to your accommodation. There are two nights in every “Provence trip” week, where it’ll be parents night. So your nanny will stay with your kids for dinner and have an activity planned while the trip director will take you to an elegant romantic delicious restaurant.

    What can I expect from the accommodation?

    Expect a luxury safe complete vacation home for you and your family. It is a two bedroom apartment with one full bathroom. Living room and dining room together with an open kitchen setting. The kitchen is well equipped, so you can cook away your secret family recipes. All linens, towels, soap, shampoo, conditioner, lotion, kids shampoo, and other basic toiletries are included. The accommodation grounds are very big, so you can go out for a walk with your little ones within without having to worry about cars or other kind of dangers. There is a playground area, petanque field and a swimming pool. If you’d like to play outside sports, we have everything, from balls to badminton rackets. You can also enjoy a nice evening near the bbq and pizza oven area.

    Is it possible to enjoy one of the days at the accommodation to just relax?

    Sure. Plenty of families that live in local cities, visit our accommodation to enjoy the nice Provence sun, pool, and do activities near by like riding bikes in the Luberon area.

    How do I sign up for a trip?

    Reserving a trip is easy! email: jessicadear@me.com with your family’s details, desired dates, and destination (Provence or the Alps). Our group sizes are small, so departures frequently sell out months in advance. Sign up as early as possible! Once you sign up and the dates, price, and details are set, we will require a $500 USD deposit per adult. Soon after you will receive a confirmation email from us. We ask that you do not purchase airline tickets until your trip has been confirmed.

    When are payments due?

    Most trips require a deposit (usually fully refundable until 90 days before departure) and a final payment two months before departure. You can make payments online.

    What forms of payment do you accept?

    All prices quoted are in euros and are subject to change if you request a bespoke trip or have some add-ons.

    Do I need a visa or passport?

    All US citizens traveling to other countries are required to have a valid passport. Many of our trips take place in countries that also require US citizens to carry a travel visa. If your trip requires a visa, information will be included in your pre-departure materials.

    Should I purchase trip cancellation insurance?

    Sometimes life happens, and you need to cancel your trip or return home early. Stroller Trips cannot issue refunds in many cases. Travel insurance helps protect against last-minute cancellation, trip interruption, and to cover medical treatment or emergency medical evacuation during your trip. We strongly recommend you purchase travel insurance in advance of your trip.

    What if Stroller Trips cancels my trip?

    Stroller Trips reserves the right to cancel all or a portion of a trip. In the event of such cancellation, full refunds will be at Stroller Trips sole discretion. Stroller Trips is not responsible for additional expenses incurred by you in preparing for the trip (including non-refundable air tickets, visa fees, gear or medical expenses, etc.).

    What if I don't need every activity/accommodation nights/etc. on my trip?

    Stroller Trips does not give any refunds for unused accommodation nights, activities, sightseeing trips or add-ons. Trip price is quoted as a package; credits are not given for services not used.

    Is airfare included in the trip price?

    Our prices are land costs only; airfare is additional.

    Can you help me book my airline tickets?

    If you're looking for the best price, we suggest contacting your preferred airlines directly or searching online for airline tickets. For the greatest convenience in making your airline arrangements, Stroller Trips also has agents on staff who can assist you with ticketing and reservations around the world for an additional fee, email us for details.
