      Visit the Alps

    Ski in the Alps

    It doesn't matter if you're an expert or a beginner, you can enjoy and learn how to skii in one of the best mountains in the world.

    Enjoy the slopes

    Aiguille Du Midi

    is a 3,843m high mountain within the Mont Blanc massif in the French Alps. The Aiguille summit contains a panoramic viewing platform, a café and a gift shop. The Vallée Blanche ski run begins here, and the nearby Cosmiques Refuge is the starting point for one of the routes to the Mont Blanc summit. The Aiguille du Midi Cable car holds the record as the highest vertical ascent cable car in the world, from 1,035 m to 3842 m.

    Live Mont-Blanc

    The Alps during the Summer

    The Alps has plenty of thrilling activities all year-round. Hiking is safe and fun even for kids. Allow your children to be in touch with nature.

    Up for a hike?

    Tour de France?

    It's not exactly "Le Tour de France", but if you're into bikes, this is a great place to train or simply ride along. The Alps has amazing mountain biking routes. You can down-hill or cross country, so get your helmet, lift pass, your bike, and let's go! Biking schools and guides available.

    On your marks, get set, sign up!

    Alpinism is in!

    whether it's a short or long mountaineering experience, it should definitely be in your bucket list. You can add-on: a private instruction course on climbing, ice climbing and / or even ascend Mont Blanc, Monte Rosa, or Gran Paradiso.

    Live it all!

    Dog Sleigh

    If you're an animal lover, you can dog sleigh, dog hike, or go horse riding in the Alps. It's one of the funnest, sweetest, most amazing experiences you'll ever have. Not only do you get to see the beautiful passages in the Alps, but you get to spend time with these wonderful animals.

    Bark or neigh?

    Train lover?.

    If you're not up for a hike, you're in luck! There are day trips to beautiful high mountain settings by train. All you have to do, is be at the station on time.

    All aboard?

    Water Sports lovers

    Canyoning, hydrospeed, fishing, rafting - You name it! The Alps has it all. So bring your swimsuit and splash away!

    Let's dive into the fun

    Outdoor Climbing

    If you're feeling like spiderman, climb away in an outdoor or indoor setting.

    Going up?

    Paragliding

    Want to fly around the mountains? The Alps is the ideal place for discovering free flying in perfect safety, thanks to the professionalism of the paragliding instructors. Courses available.

    Up in the air

    Via Ferrata

    is a rocky climbing route with safety cables. You make your way along using rungs, rope bridges and other zip-lines. A great way to conquer your fear of heights!

    Dare to walk on the cable

    Helicopter rides

    is ideal for those who are a little less daring but wish to go higher. This way you can see the legendary peaks in this rocky and icy paradise.

    Fly high

    Chamonix

    is a charming world class town, situated at the foot of western Europe's highest mountain.

    Visit Chamonix

    Family walks

    are always fun, inspiring, and pleasant. There are countless of safe routes that you and your loved ones can enjoy.

    Let's go for a stroll

    Cheese, potatoes, wine

    Savoyard cooking, the typical food from Chamonix Mont-Blanc, is based on a staple diet of cheese and potatoes. Produced locally in summer and easy to store during the long winter months, and with a high calorific value, they make for a good human fuel source.
